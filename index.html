PROGRESSION RULES

LIFTSHit top of rep range on ALL sets → add 5 lbs next session. Don't rush it.
SPEEDLog every 40 time. Improvement comes from mechanics first, not effort.
JUMPSAdd box height when you land easily and consistently. Step down every rep.
SKILLS75+ ball catches every Friday. Reps build hands. No shortcuts.
JJTap early, tap often. You're learning — getting tapped means you're rolling. Keep going.

COACHING CUES — REMEMBER THESE

40-yard startStay LOW first 10 yards — don't stand up early
Arms sprinting90 degrees, drive from hip to chin, don't cross body
SquatChest up, knees track toes, full depth every rep
CatchingTurn head early, hands catch — never body catch
Cone drillsStay low in cuts — standing up kills your time
Sleep9+ hours — this is your #1 performance tool at 12

NUTRITION BASICS FOR YOUNG ATHLETES

PROTEIN0.7–1g per lb bodyweight — chicken, eggs, beef, milk, Greek yogurt. Every meal.
CARBSEat plenty — rice, oats, potatoes, fruit. You're growing and training. Don't restrict.
WATERDrink water constantly. If you're thirsty, you're already behind. Half bodyweight in oz daily.
TIMINGEat within 30 min after training. Protein + carbs. Chocolate milk works fine post-workout.
